02 What data we collect

When you register to use Marc, we collect:

  • Full name — to personalise your session
  • Email address — to send your A100 manual PDF after Ko-fi payment, and optional product updates if you consent
  • Phone number — optional, for service centre referral follow-up only
  • Country — to connect you with the nearest authorised service centre
  • Marketing consent — only if you explicitly tick the box

We do not collect: payment details (Ko-fi handles all payments), passwords, or sensitive personal data. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising pixels.

03 How we use your data

  • To provide access to the Marc AI assistant
  • To email you the A100 manual PDF after Ko-fi payment confirmation
  • To refer you to authorised service centres in your country
  • To send product updates and service centre referrals — only if you consented
  • To improve the AirlessFix service based on usage patterns (anonymised)

We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.
